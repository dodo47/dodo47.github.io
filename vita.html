Work experience

2021 - now: Internal Research Fellow in AI.
I work as a research fellow at ESA's Advanced Concepts Team and Software Division, researching applications of AI methods - including neuromorphic computing - for space missions.

2020 - 2021: AI Residency Researcher.
I worked as a resident at the Siemens AI lab in Munich, exploring industrial applications for neuromorphic AI.

2016 - 2020: Doctoral Researcher.
Petrovici group for neuro-inspired theory, modeling and applications and Meier/Schemmel group for neuromorphic computing, Heidelberg University.

2017 - 2018: Guest Researcher.
Senn group for computational neuroscience, University of Bern. Stayed for six months with a continuing collaboration and regular visits afterwards during my PhD.

2014 - 2015: Research Assistant.
Evers group for theoretical quantum dynamics, Max-Planck-Institute for Nuclear Physics, Heidelberg. Follow-up work after the Bachelor thesis.
